Privacy Policy & Data Protection

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities

Last updated: January 2024

Data Protection Overview

HealthAI is committed to protecting your personal health information and ensuring compliance with international data protection regulations including GDPR, HIPAA, and CCPA.

End-to-End Encryption

All health data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption both in transit and at rest.

AI-Powered Security

Advanced AI chatbot provides intelligent health assistance with secure data processing.

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We use zero-knowledge proofs to verify data without accessing sensitive information.

Data We Collect

Health Information

  • Medical conditions and diagnoses
  • Medication history and adherence
  • Vital signs and lab results
  • Lifestyle and behavioral data
  • Genetic information (with consent)

Technical Data

  • Device information and sensors
  • App usage patterns
  • Performance metrics
  • Error logs (anonymized)

Personal Information

  • Contact information
  • Account preferences
  • Communication history
  • Emergency contacts

How We Use Your Data

Personalized Healthcare

Creating your digital twin and providing personalized health recommendations

AI Health Coaching

Training AI models to provide adaptive health coaching and support

Risk Prediction

Analyzing patterns to predict health risks and provide early warnings

Community Support

Facilitating peer support while maintaining privacy through AI moderation

Data Sharing & Third Parties

Healthcare Providers

We share data with your authorized healthcare providers only with your explicit consent and through secure, encrypted channels.

Research Partners

Anonymized data may be shared with research institutions for medical research, always with your consent and following strict protocols.

Emergency Situations

In life-threatening emergencies, we may share critical health information with emergency responders and designated contacts.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Binding Corporate Rules
  • Certification schemes

Data Retention

Health Records

Retained for 7 years after last activity or as required by law

Account Data

Retained until account deletion or 3 years of inactivity

Analytics Data

Anonymized and retained for 2 years for service improvement

Communication Logs

Retained for 1 year for support and quality purposes

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Report to relevant authorities within 24 hours
  • Provide detailed information about the breach
  • Offer support and remediation measures
  • Implement additional security measures
